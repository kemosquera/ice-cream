The Classic Vanilla Ice Cream

The History

Vanilla ice cream has been a beloved dessert since the 1780s when Thomas Jefferson discovered it in France. He was so impressed that he wrote down the recipe and brought it back to America, making it one of the first ice cream flavors in the United States.

Pure Madagascar Vanilla

Our vanilla ice cream uses pure Madagascar bourbon vanilla beans, known for their rich, creamy flavor profile with hints of caramel and subtle floral notes. Each bean is carefully selected and hand-processed to ensure the highest quality.

Crafting Process

We use a traditional French custard base, slowly incorporating the finest cream and letting the vanilla beans steep for 24 hours. This patient process ensures that every spoonful delivers the perfect balance of sweetness and authentic vanilla flavor.
